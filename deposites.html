    • p>ICICI Bank offers various savings options, including Fixed Deposits, Recurring Deposits, and the innovative iWish - Goal-Based Savings account.

    Fixed Deposits provide a secure way to grow savings at a fixed interest rate over a chosen tenure, typically ranging from 7 days to 10 years. This option is ideal for those seeking stable returns without market risks.

    Recurring Deposits allow customers to save a fixed amount regularly, typically monthly, helping to build a substantial corpus over time. With competitive interest rates and flexible tenures from 6 months to 10 years, this option is perfect for those who prefer disciplined saving.

    The iWish account is designed for goal-oriented saving, allowing customers to set specific financial targets, like vacations or education. Users can track their progress toward these goals, making it easier to stay motivated.

    Together, these products empower customers to save effectively and achieve their financial aspirations with ICICI Bank.
